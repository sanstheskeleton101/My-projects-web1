Undertale Guide

Undertale is an RPG game where a human has fallen into the underground the land of monsters and must survive to escape

Sans is a major character in the undertale franchise and was the first character to be designed. He is one of the boss monsters in the game if you decide to kill all the monsters in the game.

You play as a human child that has fallen into the underground. when the game starts you get to name the fallen human. If you name the fallen human frisk then the game will go into hard mode./p>
